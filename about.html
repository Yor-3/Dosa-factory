When did dosas first appear in the India?

Originating in southern India, the crispy dosa has been around since the 5th century AD. It's said that dosa was first referenced by Chalukya King Somesvara III in Tamil literature in 1054 AD. Like many Indian foods, dosa varies depending on region and occasion. . (Source: sukhis.com)
